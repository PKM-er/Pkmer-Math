∫0acos⁡θra2−r2dr\int_{0}^{a\cos\theta} r\sqrt{a^2-r^2}dr ∫0acosθ​ra2−r2​dr

你给出的积分表达式在柱坐标系下是正确的. 现在, 我们来逐步计算这个体积积分:

V=4∫0π/2dθ∫0acos⁡θr dr∫0a2−r2dzV = 4\int_{0}^{\pi/2} d\theta \int_{0}^{a\cos\theta} r \, dr \int_{0}^{\sqrt{a^2-r^2}} dz V=4∫0π/2​dθ∫0acosθ​rdr∫0a2−r2​​dz

1. 计算 zzz 积分

首先计算最内层的 zzz 积分:

∫0a2−r2dz=a2−r2\int_{0}^{\sqrt{a^2 - r^2}} dz = \sqrt{a^2 - r^2} ∫0a2−r2​​dz=a2−r2​

所以积分式变为:

V=4∫0π/2dθ∫0acos⁡θra2−r2 drV = 4\int_{0}^{\pi/2} d\theta \int_{0}^{a\cos\theta} r\sqrt{a^2 - r^2} \, dr V=4∫0π/2​dθ∫0acosθ​ra2−r2​dr

2. 计算 rrr 积分

我们接下来计算 rrr 积分. 设 u=a2−r2u = a^2 - r^2u=a2−r2, 则 du=−2r drdu = -2r \, drdu=−2rdr:

∫ra2−r2 dr=−12∫u du=−12⋅23u3/2+C=−13(a2−r2)3/2\int r\sqrt{a^2 - r^2} \, dr = -\frac{1}{2} \int \sqrt{u} \, du = -\frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{2}{3} u^{3/2} + C = -\frac{1}{3}(a^2 - r^2)^{3/2} ∫ra2−r2​dr=−21​∫u​du=−21​⋅32​u3/2+C=−31​(a2−r2)3/2

将 rrr 的上下限代入:

∫0acos⁡θra2−r2 dr=[−13(a2−r2)3/2]0acos⁡θ\int_{0}^{a\cos\theta} r\sqrt{a^2 - r^2} \, dr = \left[-\frac{1}{3}(a^2 - r^2)^{3/2}\right]_{0}^{a\cos\theta} ∫0acosθ​ra2−r2​dr=[−31​(a2−r2)3/2]0acosθ​

=−13[(a2−(acos⁡θ)2)3/2−(a2−02)3/2]= -\frac{1}{3}\left[(a^2 - (a\cos\theta)^2)^{3/2} - (a^2 - 0^2)^{3/2}\right] =−31​[(a2−(acosθ)2)3/2−(a2−02)3/2]

=−13[(a2(1−cos⁡2θ))3/2−a3]= -\frac{1}{3}\left[(a^2(1 - \cos^2\theta))^{3/2} - a^3\right] =−31​[(a2(1−cos2θ))3/2−a3]

=−13[(a2sin⁡2θ)3/2−a3]= -\frac{1}{3}\left[(a^2\sin^2\theta)^{3/2} - a^3\right] =−31​[(a2sin2θ)3/2−a3]

=−a33[sin⁡3θ−1]= -\frac{a^3}{3}\left[\sin^3\theta - 1\right] =−3a3​[sin3θ−1]

所以积分式变为:

V=4∫0π/2−a33(sin⁡3θ−1)dθV = 4\int_{0}^{\pi/2} -\frac{a^3}{3} (\sin^3\theta - 1) d\theta V=4∫0π/2​−3a3​(sin3θ−1)dθ

3. 计算 θ\thetaθ 积分

现在, 我们计算最后的 θ\thetaθ 积分:

V=−4a33∫0π/2(sin⁡3θ−1)dθV = -\frac{4a^3}{3} \int_{0}^{\pi/2} (\sin^3\theta - 1) d\theta V=−34a3​∫0π/2​(sin3θ−1)dθ

这可以分解为两个积分:

V=−4a33[∫0π/2sin⁡3θ dθ−∫0π/21 dθ]V = -\frac{4a^3}{3} \left[\int_{0}^{\pi/2} \sin^3\theta \, d\theta - \int_{0}^{\pi/2} 1 \, d\theta \right] V=−34a3​[∫0π/2​sin3θdθ−∫0π/2​1dθ]

  1. 计算 ∫0π/21 dθ=π2\int_{0}^{\pi/2} 1 \, d\theta = \frac{\pi}{2}∫0π/2​1dθ=2π​
  2. 对于 ∫sin⁡3θ dθ\int \sin^3\theta \, d\theta∫sin3θdθ, 我们可以使用三角函数的恒等式:

sin⁡3θ=3sin⁡θ−sin⁡(3θ)4\sin^3\theta = \frac{3\sin\theta - \sin(3\theta)}{4} sin3θ=43sinθ−sin(3θ)​

因此:

∫0π/2sin⁡3θ dθ=14[3∫0π/2sin⁡θ dθ−∫0π/2sin⁡(3θ) dθ]\int_{0}^{\pi/2} \sin^3\theta \, d\theta = \frac{1}{4}\left[3\int_{0}^{\pi/2} \sin\theta \, d\theta - \int_{0}^{\pi/2} \sin(3\theta) \, d\theta \right] ∫0π/2​sin3θdθ=41​[3∫0π/2​sinθdθ−∫0π/2​sin(3θ)dθ]

使用标准积分公式得到:

∫sin⁡θ dθ=−cos⁡θ,∫sin⁡(3θ) dθ=−13cos⁡(3θ)\int \sin\theta \, d\theta = -\cos\theta, \quad \int \sin(3\theta) \, d\theta = -\frac{1}{3}\cos(3\theta) ∫sinθdθ=−cosθ,∫sin(3θ)dθ=−31​cos(3θ)

积分结果为:

∫0π/2sin⁡3θ dθ=14[3(1−0)−13(1−1)]=34\int_{0}^{\pi/2} \sin^3\theta \, d\theta = \frac{1}{4}\left[3(1 - 0) - \frac{1}{3}(1 - 1)\right] = \frac{3}{4} ∫0π/2​sin3θdθ=41​[3(1−0)−31​(1−1)]=43​

  1. 将以上结果代入体积公式:

V=−4a33(34−π2)=4a33(π2−34)V = -\frac{4a^3}{3} \left(\frac{3}{4} - \frac{\pi}{2}\right) = \frac{4a^3}{3} \left(\frac{\pi}{2} - \frac{3}{4}\right) V=−34a3​(43​−2π​)=34a3​(2π​−43​)

最终结果为:

V=4a33(π2−34)V = \frac{4a^3}{3} \left(\frac{\pi}{2} - \frac{3}{4}\right) V=34a3​(2π​−43​)

这就是你所求的体积表达式.

到这一步, 我们的答案是一样的: $$ V = 4\int_{0}^{\pi/2} d\theta \int_{0}^{a\cos\theta} r\sqrt{a^2 - r^2} , dr $$ 但是在这之后, 我用参数方程x=rcos⁡θx=r\cos\thetax=rcosθ和y=rsin⁡θy=r\sin\thetay=rsinθ带入圆柱方程x2+y2=ax, 从而得到r=acos⁡θr=a\cos\thetar=acosθ, 再将这个结果带回上面的式子, a2−r2\sqrt{a^2 - r^2}a2−r2​就变成了asin⁡θa\sin\thetaasinθ, 然后算出了一个完全不同的结果, 这样对吗?

你所采用的方法是正确的, 并且这种方法可能会给出一个更简单的表达式. 让我们一步步核对并验证你的计算.

1. 使用参数方程 x=rcos⁡θx = r\cos\thetax=rcosθ 和 y=rsin⁡θy = r\sin\thetay=rsinθ

将这些参数方程代入圆柱面方程 x2+y2=axx^2 + y^2 = axx2+y2=ax:

r2=arcos⁡θr^2 = a r \cos\theta r2=arcosθ
